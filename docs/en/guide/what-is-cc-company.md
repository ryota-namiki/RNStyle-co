# What is cc-company?

cc-company is a plugin that lets you build and run a **virtual organization** on Claude Code.

## Concept

```
You → Secretary (gateway) → Departments (added as needed)
```

- The **secretary** is always your point of contact
- You can consult the secretary about anything — TODO management, brainstorming, notes
- As work increases, the secretary suggests adding departments

## Why a Secretary-Centered Design?

Traditional organization management tools require you to define the organizational structure before you start. But for individuals and small teams, there's no need to set up all departments from the beginning.

cc-company starts with **"just a secretary."**

- When tasks pile up → The secretary manages your TODOs
- When an idea strikes → The secretary takes notes
- When you want to brainstorm → The secretary becomes your sounding board
- When research is needed → The secretary suggests: "Shall I create a Research department?"

**The organization grows naturally as you use it.** That's the design philosophy of cc-company.

## Who is it for?

- Solo developers
- Freelancers
- Side projects & startups
- Anyone who wants to organize their daily tasks and information

By adding departments, you can scale to full organizational management — project management, marketing, accounting, sales, and more.
